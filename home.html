    About Nordic Tree Atlas

    Welcome to the Nordic Tree Atlas! This platform serves as a comprehensive guide to the diverse tree species found in the Nordic region. Explore information about various trees, their characteristics, and their ecological significance.

    Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a student, or someone interested in the rich biodiversity of Nordic forests, this atlas provides an introduction to some of the prominent tree species, including Spruce, Pine, Birch, and Oak.

    Feel free to navigate through the different sections to learn more about each tree. Use the search bar to find specific information or browse the tree species listed in the left menu.
